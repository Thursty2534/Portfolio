Our Story

A love letter to
Mexican tradition

Desde México con amor

Brasa was born from a single, stubborn belief: that Dubai deserved tacos made the way they are in Mexico — slowly, honestly, with reverence for the ingredient.

We source heirloom corn directly from Mexican farms in Chiapas and Guerrero, nixtamalise and grind it in-house each morning, then hand-press every tortilla to order. Our meats are wood-fired, our salsas ground fresh daily, our aguas frescas made from stone fruit and dried flowers.

This is not a chain. This is a cantina. Come hungry, stay late.

The Craft

Made the
old way

In Mexico, cooking is not a shortcut. It is a ceremony. We honour that ceremony every single day inside our open kitchen.

Our heirloom corn travels directly from small farms in Chiapas — the same varieties cultivated for millennia. We nixtamalise the kernels overnight in cal, grind them at dawn, and your tortilla is pressed from living masa, never from a package.

🌽

Heirloom Corn

Sourced directly from Chiapas & Guerrero — ancient varieties you won't find in a supermarket.

🔥

Wood Fire

Every protein meets the wood-fired grill. No gas. The smoke is not decoration.

🫙

Nixtamalisation

We soak and cook our corn in cal each night. The masa is ground in-house, pressed to order.
