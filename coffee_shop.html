Where It All Starts

We travel to source. Every bean is traceable to a single farm, a single harvest, a single person who cares as much as we do.

01 01

Ethiopia

Yirgacheffe · Natural Process · 1,900 m

The birthplace of coffee — and still one of its finest. Our Yirgacheffe comes from a cooperative of 12 families. Dried on raised beds under high-altitude sun for 28 days. Untouched. Unrushed.

Blueberry Dark Chocolate Jasmine
Light Roast · Single Origin
02 02

Colombia

Huila · Washed Process · 1,850 m

18 months of direct relationships with three families above the Magdalena River. A washed process that strips the fruit back and reveals the pure, structured sweetness of the bean itself.

Caramel Red Apple Hazelnut
Medium Roast · Single Origin
03 03

Sumatra

Mandheling · Wet-Hulled · 1,500 m

The most different cup in our rotation. The wet-hull process unique to Sumatra creates a full-bodied, earthy bean unlike anything grown elsewhere. Not for everyone. Perfect for some.

Dark Chocolate Cedar Tobacco
Dark Roast · Espresso Blend
Nothing Complicated

From Farm to Cup

Four steps. No shortcuts. The same process for every single order, every single day.

01

Source

We travel to origin, cup with farmers, and buy directly. No middlemen. Full traceability from seed to bag.

02

Roast

Small batches in our Dubai roast room. Each origin gets its own profile, developed over months of cupping.

03

Grind

Freshly ground to order, calibrated to your brew method. Never pre-ground. Never compromised.

04

Brew

Your method, our full attention. Espresso, V60, Chemex, AeroPress — executed with precision and care.
